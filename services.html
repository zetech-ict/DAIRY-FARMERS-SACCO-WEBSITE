More about us!!!

1.Agro vet shop for provision of animal feeds and VET items on credit and cash basis, the society has five Agro vet shops at Ndunyu Njeru, Engineer, Munyaka, kwaharaka, Miharati and Shamata. The society is in the process of opening two other new shops at Olkalou and Murungaru areas.


2.The society also engages service providers where it is not able to reach the members who offer the same services on credit and the society pays them at the end of the month.


3.Artificial Insemination (AI). This facility is offered both on credit and cash basis this is in line with the continuity on breed improvement to maximize on production.


4.Training of Farmers. The current average production is 9 kgs per day, per member. The society aims to improve this to 20 kgs per day through training and extension services the farmers trainings and extensions are conducted by the society for free to the members. Extension services also offers capacity building to the members on dairy production skills, breeding, feeds and feeding, dairy management and milk quality production.


5.Interest free Cash emergency advances to farmers who have needs and may not wait until the end of the month.


6.The society through check off facility provides National Hospital Insurance Fund (NHIF) for the members.


7.High quality Dairy meal formulation that is sold to the members and non members at an affordable price.


8.Milk Bonus. At the end of the year, the society distributes the available surplus to the farmers in form of bonus per every kilo delivered in the year.


9.Availing quality farm inputs and machineries to the members to purchase on credit on check off system or on cash basis.


10.Milk collection, grading, transport and marketing through the Kinangop Dairy ltd.
